FAQ

How Referrals Work.

How does $10/month work exactly? ▼
For every person who signs up through your referral link and stays active, you earn $10/month in credits. Credits are reviewed monthly and applied toward your own Gulf Coast Mow bill first. Earnings beyond your bill are processed up to the $600/year cap.
When do credits get applied? ▼
Credits are reviewed during the first week of each month and applied as a Stripe billing adjustment on your next bill.
Why the $600/year cap? ▼
The IRS requires a 1099 for payments to individuals over $600/year. To keep things simple during our launch phase, we cap referral earnings at $600/year per person.
Do I need to be a customer to refer? ▼
No — anyone can join the referral program. You don't need to be a lawn service customer yourself.
