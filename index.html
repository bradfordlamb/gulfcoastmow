FAQ

Every Question Answered.

How does $10/month work exactly? ▼
For every person who signs up through your referral link and remains an active subscriber, you earn $10 flat each calendar month. It doesn't matter if they're on the $45/week or $60/week plan — you always earn $10/month per active yard. If a referral cancels, that $10 stops. If they resubscribe, it starts again automatically.
When and how do I get paid? ▼
Payouts are processed automatically on the 1st of every month for the previous month's active referrals. You can receive payment via PayPal or direct bank deposit (ACH). You'll get a payout summary email each month showing exactly which referrals earned what.
How are referrals tracked? Won't I lose credit? ▼
We use Tolt (tolt.io), a dedicated affiliate tracking platform built for Stripe subscriptions. When someone clicks your link, a 30-day cookie is set. If they sign up anytime within that 30 days, you get permanent credit for that customer. Tolt connects directly to our Stripe account so there's no manual tracking — it's all automatic and auditable. You can see every referral's status in your dashboard at any time.
What if my referral pauses or cancels? ▼
You only earn on active months. If a referral pauses or cancels, the $10 stops for that period. No clawbacks on months already paid — those are yours to keep. If they resubscribe later, the earnings resume automatically. You don't need to do anything.
Is there a limit on how many people I can refer? ▼
No limit whatsoever. Refer 1 yard or 100 yards — the $10/month rate applies equally to every active referral. There are no tiers, no caps, and no diminishing returns.
Do I need to be a Gulf Coast Mow customer to refer? ▼
No. You can join the referral program without being a lawn service customer. Anyone can refer — neighbors, friends, local community members, real estate agents, or anyone who knows homeowners in our service area.
Can I refer myself or someone in my household? ▼
No. Self-referrals are not permitted and are automatically flagged in the system. The referral program is designed for referring other households only. Violations result in removal from the program.
What if someone I referred signed up without using my link? ▼
Unfortunately, we can only track referrals that come through your unique link — that's what makes automatic payouts possible. We'd encourage you to share your link directly rather than just mentioning the service, since the link is what connects the signup to your account.

FAQ

Questions From Crews.

How much do I get paid per job? ▼
Pay per job is discussed during onboarding and depends on yard size and your area. We structure it so you keep the majority of the job revenue — we take a cut to cover customer acquisition, billing, and platform costs. You'll know the rate before you agree to any route.
Do I need to download an app? ▼
No app required. We send your weekly schedule by text every Monday. When a job is done, you text us one photo of the completed lawn and we release your payment. That's it.
How does Stripe work — is it safe? ▼
Stripe is one of the largest and most trusted payment platforms in the world — used by Amazon, Lyft, Instacart, and millions of businesses. Your bank info is encrypted and never shared. The setup takes about 5 minutes and is completely secure.
Will I get a 1099 at the end of the year? ▼
If your total payments exceed $600 in a calendar year, Stripe automatically generates and sends you a 1099-K for tax purposes. This is standard for independent contractors. Stripe handles the paperwork — you don't need to do anything.
Can I pick which days or areas I work? ▼
Yes — during onboarding we'll ask which zip codes you can cover and what days work for you. We try to build routes that match your availability. The more flexible you are, the more jobs we can send your way.
What if I can't make a scheduled job? ▼
Text us as early as possible — at least 24 hours before the scheduled mow if you can. We'll reassign the job. Reliability is the most important thing to us and to our customers, so consistent no-shows will affect whether we can keep you on the route.
